Our Story

Fijian Luxury Awaits

Escape to DoubleTree Resort by Hilton Fiji, located on the stunning Sonaisali Island. Just a short drive from Nadi International Airport, our resort offers a blend of traditional Fijian culture and modern Hilton comfort.

Start your journey with our signature warm chocolate chip cookie and end it with memories that last a lifetime. Whether you seek adventure or relaxation, our island sanctuary is the perfect destination.
